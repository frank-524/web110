Enlightenment MMA

With so much misinformation spread about MMA and martial arts, Enlightenment MMA was created to enlighten people about MMA and martial arts! Hopefully, after visiting this site, you will feel inspired to try a martial art. Then when you feel ready, you can transition to the sport of MMA.

MMA is challenging and may seem unsafe. However, under the right guidelines, MMA is a safe sport. A common myth about MMA is that there are no rules. This myth is not true; MMA is a heavily regulated sport with many benefits!

This website will focus on six main martial arts categories. With MMA being under the hybrid page. We classified MMA as a hybrid fighting system since it is not a martial art. Moreover, we classified each martial art with its most accurate category.
