Grappling/ground

Grappling or ground fighting focuses on taking an opponent down on the floor to achieve a dominant position or to end the fight with a submission. Techniques used in grappling/ground martial arts include takedowns, chokes, arm locks, leg locks, throws, and more!

Folkstyle Wrestling

There are many forms of wrestling out there. The four main styles of wrestling out there are folkstyle, freestyle, Greco-Roman, and catch wrestling. Each style of wrestling has a unique set of rules.

We will focus on folkstyle wrestling. Folkstyle wrestling (aka scholastic wrestling) is a wrestling style you will see in a high school and college level. Folkstyle focuses on takedowns on the feet; and controlled riding and turns while on top. Wrestlers can not lock hands unless it is a pinning combination or a takedown attempt.

The goal of folkstyle wrestling is to pin your opponent using takedowns, throws, and pins. A pin is holding your opponent back for two seconds. Folkstyle wrestling is perfect to learn how to takedown an opponent!

Rules and Equipment

What is allowed? Takedowns, grappling, throws, and clinching are allowed.
Equipment: Wrestling shoes, mouthguard, headgear, and wrestling singlet.
What is not allowed? Punches, kicks, joint locks, and chokes are not allowed.

Prominent Figures

  • Cael Sanderson
  • Dan Gable
  • Cain Velasquez
  • Ben Askren

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses on ways how to submit an opponent. Since it is easier to control on the ground; much of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu takes place on the ground. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaches how to takedown an opponent and wrestle for a dominant position.

However, unlike folkstyle wrestling, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu allows chokes and joint locks. Common Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques include armbar, guillotine, and triangle. If you want to learn how to fight on your back Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is for you! You will learn many grappling positions along with chokes and joint locks.

Rules and Equipment

What is allowed? Takedowns, throws, clinching, joint locks, choking are allowed.
Equipment: Gi (outfit), belt, rash guard, and mouthguard.
What is not allowed? Punches and kicks are not allowed.

Prominent Figures

  • Royce Gracie
  • Eddie Bravo
  • Rubens Charles Maciel
  • Demian Maia

Catch Wrestling

Jiu-Jitsu translates to gentle art. Catch wrestling is known as the violent art. Catch wrestling is a hybrid grappling style that allows wrestling and submission. In catch wrestling, there are no limits on how you can manipulate the body.

Catch wrestling is a wrestling based background so it starts on the feet. Some Jiu-Jitsu classes frown upon and forbid the use of some techniques like spine locks, neck cranks, leglocks, and slams. These techniques are allowed in catch wrestling.

Unfortunately, not many people know about catch wrestling because of the lack of structure and certified coaches.

Rules and Equipment

What is allowed? Takedowns, throws, clinching, joint locks, and chokes(sometimes).
Equipment: Wrestling shoes, mouthguard, and headgear.
What is not allowed? Punches and kicks are not allowed.

Prominent Figures

  • Josh Barnett
  • Randy Couture
