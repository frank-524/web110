M4A Review a Site

The website that I will be reviewing is the House of Eight Limbs website

  • What is the company/organization? The company is a Muay Thai gym
  • What is the URL? House Of Eight Limbs Website
  • What is the purpose of the site? The purpose of the site is to inform the audience where they can go for Muay Thai classes.
  • Who is the intended audience? The intended audience are males/females ages 7 +. Any martial arts experience can join.
  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience? Yes, I believe the site reaches its audience. When I type "Kickboxing gym or MMA gym" the House Of Eight Limbs website shows up.
  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not? Yes, the site was useful. The site answers all my questions and I know what they do. In fact, I actually attended some of their classes!
  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website: The website use white as a background color and black for the text. On the top left corner is their logo. The home page includes pictures of the gym.
