Web Site Goal

The goal of the website is to inform the audience about MMA and the different types of martial arts.

Working Titles

  • Martial Arts Categories (drop down menu)

Forms

There will be a frequently asked questions page aka FAQ page. In this page the user can enter their email and pick their favorite martial art(s).

Sources
