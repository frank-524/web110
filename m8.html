Chicken Quesadillas

Chicken Quesadillas
Ingredients Quantity
Extra-virgin olive-oil 1 tbsp.
Bell peppers, thinly sliced 2
Onion, thinly sliced 1/2
Kosher Salt 1
Boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced 1lb
Chili powder 1/2 tsp.
Ground cumin 1/2 tsp.
Dried oregano 1/2 tsp.
Medium flour tortillas 4
Shredded Monterey jack 2 c.
Sliced avocado 1
Vegetable oil 1 tbsp.
Green onions, thinly sliced 2
Sour Cream 1 tbsp.
Instructions Step
Add olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add peppers, onion, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes. Step 1
Heat vegetable oil (medium-high heat) and season chicken with spices,salt, and pepper. Cook the chicken and stir occasionally, until golden. Do this for 8 minutes then transfer to a plate. Step 2
Add 1 flour tortilla to skillet and add the top half of the tortilla with cheeses, chicken mixture, pepper-onion mixture, a few slices of avocado, and green onions. Fold the other half of the tortilla over and cook 3 minutes per side. Repeat to make 4 quesadillas. Step 3
Slice into wedges and serve with sour cream. Step 4
